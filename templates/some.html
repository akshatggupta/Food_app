Food Waste Around the World

Every year, 1.3 billion tons of food are wasted globally, which is about one-third of all food produced for human consumption. This wastage is not only a moral issue but also an environmental one. Wasting food contributes to climate change, as it requires resources like water, land, and energy to produce, process, and transport food that ends up in landfills. By reducing food waste and redistributing leftovers, we can help fight hunger and protect the planet.

How We Work

We partner with restaurants, catering services, PGs, and messes to collect leftover food, which is then either given away for free or at a reduced price to NGOs. You can pick up the food directly, or we can arrange for a delivery within a 500-meter radius through a delivery partner.
